    Projects

  • Boijmans

    Boijmans is a visual arts museum based in Rotterdam.

    I collaborated on the Depot page which showcases the progress of the first art storage facility in the world that offers access to a museum’s complete collection.

    My main task was to develop the timeline items.

    I've also created the donation page, which allows people to support the construction of the Depot.

  • Tilt

    Tilt is a new-media platform of the Insititute for Sound and Vision.

    The platform's purpose is to inspire readers through stories that share a connection between humans, media and community.

    I worked on the front-end logic and styling of the card customisation, allowing editors to customize their stories to their needs.

    An example of this customisation is that it allows editors to combine a wide range of UI combinations.

  • Sweet & Sunny

    Work on making multiple seasonal websites for The Greenery, these projects are made for seasonal product promotion.

    Projects are made with blade templates and a headless wordpress setup, ES6 and SASS for the interactivity within the pages, like the sliders.

    Great fun to work on these projects, since they're so colourful and quick to make.

    Links: Sweet & Sunny, Sweet Sensation, Tomatenzwerge

  • HRO

    My first ever project, a good friend of mine was HRO's student of the year and gave me the opportunity to help out with the Hogeschool's digital position paper.

    Being able to help out on this project was my first real introduction to web development / front-end.

  • SentOne

    SentOne is a company that specialises into ground works such as soundwalls, golfcourses and other projects.

    I worked on this website during my internship, the goal was to provide a clear first impression and an easier way to read about their services.
