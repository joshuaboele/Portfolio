  • Tilt

    I worked on a project for the Insitute for Sound and Vision, called Tilt.

    The idea behind this project was to be able to showcase news in a new way.

    It has an app-like interface which allows users (web & mobile) to navigate through stories, featuring audio/visuals and text.

    I've mainly contributed to making the 'cards' customizable, allowing the creator to freely choose how they want a card to look (example: Text on top, text bottom, countless of combinations).
